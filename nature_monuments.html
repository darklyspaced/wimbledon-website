Wimbledon Common

Wimbledon Common has lots of things to do from sailing to kayaking. There are many other different water sports available as well as it being a lovely space for a picnic.

Cannizaro Park

Cannizaro Park is a nice quaint gated park with lovely trails and sparse forests that are wonderful to take a stroll through and take a minute among your hectic travel plans

War Memorial

The War Memorial is located in the heart of Wimbledon Village and pays respect to all our fellow fallen. It is a nice, respectful visit to make when you just need time to reflect and take a moment.
